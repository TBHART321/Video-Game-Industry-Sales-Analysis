Overview:

Analysis of 16,000+ video games sales records exploring platform performance, genre popularity, publisher dominance, and regional market differences across North America, Europe, and Japan.

Tools Used:

MySQL, Power BI

Dataset:

vgsales dataset from Kaggle containing global video game sales data across platforms, genres, and publishers.

Analysis Questions:

What are the best selling video games of all time?
Which platforms generated the most total sales?
Which genres and publishers dominated the market?
How do regional tastes differ between North America, Europe, and Japan?

Key Findings (at the time of the dataset):

PS2 is the highest grossing platform with over 1.2 billion in total sales. Nintendo leads publishers
in total sales despite having far fewer games than competitors like EA, showing superior efficiency per title. 
Action is the dominant genre globally but Role-Playing games are the top genre in Japan. Shooters are hugely popular in North America and Europe but newarly absent in Japan.

Skills Demonstrated:

SQL - querying in MySQL including GROUP BY, SUM, COUNT, ORDER BY.
Regional and comparative data analysis.
Data visualization in Power BI.
Insight communication through data storytelling.
